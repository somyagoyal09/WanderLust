Introducing Airbnb Rooms, the vacation rentals' star offering

Travel with a twist
By anushka shah

If you’re an avid globetrotter, whose agenda is to bag rich and memorable experiences while going light on the pocket, you’re no stranger to the world of Airbnbs. Enriching agendas across maps, Airbnb’s concept of living in someone else’s home now takes an all-new and personal spin with the launch of - Airbnb Rooms! This original idea of sharing a home extends two A1 benefits for guests. First, travellers can save the coin; no bulky expenses on full-sized apartments or swanky hotels and secondly, staying with their host allows them to immerse themselves in their destination’s vibe and culture. And at what cost? Globally last year, more than 80% of private accommodations were under $100 a night (approx under INR 8,200 a night), with the average rate of a private room in India as INR 2,572 per night.

All things Airbnb Rooms

Tagging on the two perks, Airbnb Rooms has piqued everyone’s interest, framing it as one of the most popular types of stay for guests. So much so, it is the third most booked option since the launch of Airbnb Categories in May 2022. To back the numerous bookings with data, Airbnb reveals - nights stayed in India’s private room listings grew by over 80% in 2022 compared to 2021. This increase can be attributed to the affordability and the unique stay type offered by the host. Uniting the best of both worlds, a closer touch to every city along with a fuller wallet, Airbnb room poses as an attractive option for solo travellers, duos or millennials.

And to keep them all spoiled for choice, there are over 1 million room listings across the globe, with redesigned filters and added new privacy features - all to enhance the overall guest experience. So, whether you’re looking for a space in a bustling city or one closer to the serene mountains, you can always choose your best pick with Airbnb Rooms.

To elevate the experience, Airbnb recently announced that, going forward, every Airbnb Room will feature a Host Passport, which will help guests get to know their host before booking their stay.

What’s in it for the host?

If you’re looking for a passive source of income, let us tell you hosting an Airbnb Room can do more than just that. It serves as an economic lifeline for many. To an extent, in 2022 private room hosts in India earned over INR 1 Billion, with absolute five-star guest ratings. Acting a win-win for all, travel with Airbnb just gets better and my next vacation and I are here for it!
